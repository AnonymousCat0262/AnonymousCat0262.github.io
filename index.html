Dear Aflie,

(September 2018 - February 6th, 2021)

I love you, Alfie. You were a sweet angel, who fought through the death of your brother and an unfortunate skin condition. Despite all this, you were a happy and gentle boy. May you forever Rest In Peace.

Dear Eddie,

(September 2018 - October 12th, 2020)

Goodbye, my sweet boy. You were a little over two years old. I am glad to know that you passed peacefully in your sleep, with your brother right there, comforting you. I love you unimaginably so and you made my life brighter. Like losing any pet, it is like losing a family member, a very dear one who never gets angry at you and is always there for you. I will miss you Eddie. Rest in Peace.
